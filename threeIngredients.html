Simple Omelette

Ingredients Instructions
Eggs
1 Whisk eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
Cheese
2 Add cheese on one side of the omelette.
Spinach
3 Add spinach on the same side of the omelette as cheese.
4 Fold the omelette in half and cook until set and golden brown.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients Instructions
Bread

Avocado

Salt
3 Sprinkle with salt to taste.

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients Instructions
Bananas
1 Mash bananas until smooth
2 Add eggs and baking powder, mix well
3 Pour batter onto skillet, cook until golden brown
Eggs
1 Crack eggs into a bowl
2 Beat eggs until well mixed
3 Use in recipe as needed.
Baking Powder
1 Measure baking powder according to recipe
2 Add to dry ingredients
3 Mix thoroughly before using
